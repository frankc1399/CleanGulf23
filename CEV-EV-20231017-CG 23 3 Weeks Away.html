The CLEAN GULF event is in three weeks and we have not seen you register!
Now is the time to register for CLEAN GULF and commit to joining your friends and colleagues working in the preparedness and response industry, November 7-9 in San Antonio, TX. CLEAN GULF is a great opportunity to build relationships, discover real-world solutions for evolving environmental emergencies, and source new products, services, and equipment.

Registration rates increase after November 3rd. You will save both money, and time onsite, if you pre-register for CLEAN GULF. Click here to begin the quick registration process.
Need more reasons to register for CLEAN GULF?
  • You will be joining your peers from oil & gas, maritime, rail, environmental, regulatory agencies, oil spill response organizations, and service companies to share information and ideas on a variety of topics that effect the oil & gas industry in the Gulf
  • Explore the exhibit hall with over 100 companies showcasing the latest technologies and equipment for prevention, preparedness, response, and recovery.
  • Educational sessions are practical and cover a wide-variety of topics and issues that are designed to enhance your competency in preparing, responding to, and recovering from, environmental emergencies and your compliance with relevant regulations
  • You can network with your peers away from the stressful challenges of a response and build important relationships with people you might work with during a future incident
  • New to the industry? We’re offering a new response professionals program on Tuesday, November 7th. You will spend the day with fellow newbies, participating in workshops and forums, and end your day at the Welcome Reception.
